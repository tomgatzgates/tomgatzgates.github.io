CLAG — FIELD MANUAL

Objective

Win exactly the number of tricks you bid — no more, no fewer. Highest score after the final deal wins.

The deal

3–7 players, standard 52-card pack, aces high. The number of cards dealt changes each round:

  • Standard (Foss, 20 rounds): deal 1 card up to 7, six special deals, then 7 back down to 1.
  • Extended (McLeod, 22 rounds): as above with eight special deals in the middle.
  • Scottish (18 rounds): deal 7 down to 1, four special deals, then 1 back up to 7 — so the big, skilful hands fall at the end.
  • Quick (13 rounds): 1→5, three specials, 5→1.

After each deal the next card is turned to set the trump suit (except where a special deal says otherwise).

Bidding

Starting with eldest hand (left of the dealer) and going clockwise, each player nominates how many tricks they expect to win. The dealer bids last and may not make the bids add up to the number of cards dealt — so it is impossible for everyone to make their bid.

Play

Eldest hand leads. Follow suit if you can; otherwise play anything. A trick is won by the highest trump played, or if none, the highest card of the suit led. The winner leads to the next trick.

Scoring — two variations

ENGLISH (Foss / Wikipedia)
  • Bid made exactly: 10 + 2 per trick (bid 2, take 2 → 14).
  • Bid missed: 0 — trick points are lost entirely.
PAGAT (McLeod)
  • 1 point for every trick won, always.
  • Bid made exactly: +10 bonus (bid 2, take 2 → 12).
  • Bid missed: you still keep 1/trick (bid 3, take 2 → 2).

Special deals

  • No Trumps: no trump suit — highest card of the led suit wins.
  • Guess 'Em / Guess Trumps: bid before the trump suit is revealed.
  • Blind: bid before looking at your cards.
  • Misère: everyone is forced to bid 0. Take no tricks → +10; otherwise lose the deal's penalty per trick (−2, or −3 in the trumped Foss misère).
  • Most Tricks: no bidding — the sole player with the most tricks scores 10; a tie for most scores nobody anything.
  • Roll 'Em, Twos Wild, Aces Low, Dealer Calls: play twists that don't change scoring.
